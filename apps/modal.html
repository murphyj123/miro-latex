Keyboard Shortcuts

Shift + EnterNew line (renders on separate line)
Ctrl/Cmd + EnterPlace on board
TabInsert 2 spaces (indent)
Ctrl/Cmd + LOpen formula library

Tips

  • Top bar: formatting (bold, fractions, brackets, tables...)
  • Second bar: symbol & template dropdowns
  • Use & to align columns in aligned/array
  • Wrap normal text in \text{...}
